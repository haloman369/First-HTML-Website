Brief History of AOL and Why They Were so Popular

Authored By: Daniel Drechsel

AOL was the most popuar website in the nineties with over 783 million users. They were a company based out of NYC specailizing in web portal and online service provider. You originally had to use a dial connection to get to the internet. AOL launched serveral services with other companies during this decade like National Geographic, the Smithsonian and NPR. In January of the year 2000, AOL and Time Warner Cable combined to create AOL Time Warner. AOL was one of the earliest pioneers of the internet and was one one of the most iconic websites in the Unites States.
