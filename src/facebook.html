Brief History of Facebook and Why They Were so Popular

Authored By: Daniel Drechsel

THE 00s

Facebook was the third most popular site in the 2000s with over 4 billion page views. Facebook was created by Mark Zuckerberg and launched on February 4, 2004 and it was a social networking service. It was originally only for college students but by 2006 anyone with a valid email address could become a user. By December of 2005 Facebook had 6 millions users.

THE Teens

Facebook still held its spot at 3rd of the most popular sites in the Teens decade with over 24 trillion page views. In february of 2011 Facebook became the largest online photo site. In 2011 over 350 millions users were using facebook on their phones which accounted for 33% of all Facebook traffic. Facebook still serves as the most popular social networking site which has caused lots of controversy over data breaches and politics.
