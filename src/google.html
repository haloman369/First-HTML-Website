Brief History of Google and Why They Were so Popular

Authored By: Daniel Drechsel

The 00s

Google was the second most popular site in the world in the 2000s with over 9 billion users. Google was founded by Sergey Brin and Larry Page in 1998 and was a search engine. Google exploded when it became the client search engine for Yahoo.com which was the most popular site in the world at the time. By 2004 users were searching on google 200 million times per day.

THE Teens

Google became the most popular site in the world and still is the most popular site in the world with over 76 Trillion page views. By the end of 2011 google was handling 3 million search requests per day. It became so popular that the term, to google became a popular expression on the internet.
