Brief History of Yahoo and Why They Were so Popular

Authored By: Daniel Drechsel

THE 90s

During the nineties Yahoo.com was the thrid most popular website with over 308 million users. In 1994 Jerry Yang and David Flo created a website called, "Jerry and David's guide to the World Wide Web." This site was a directory of other websites organized in a heirarchy. Later on in 1994 they changed the name from, Jerry and David's guide to the World Wide Web" to "Yahoo!." The offcial Yahoo.com domain was created on January 18th of 1995. Its wild that this company was the thrid most popular given how late into the decade it was created. Yahoo was another search engine to come around during the nineties. By 1998 Yahoo became many users homepage with 95 millions views per day.

THE 00s

By the end of the 2000 decade, Yahoo became the most popular site in the world with over 11 billion users. Yahoo began using google for searching as it developed its own searching algorith over the next 4 years. When google launched gmail, yahoo started to offer unlimited email storage in 2007. In Febuary of 2008, Microsoft tried to buy yahoo for 44.6 billion but Yahoo rejected the offer saying Microsoft was substantially undervaluing the company.
